Why Now

No innovation, growing pressure

No fundamental change in 20+ years
RH probes (ASTM F2170, 2002) and calcium chloride tests (ASTM F1869, 1998) still require drilling and days of waiting.
Nobody wants to own it
Testing is multi-day, multi-visit, and error-prone. Subs try to push it to someone else.
Schedules can't absorb it
A 4-inch slab can take months to dry. As project timelines compress, there's less room for mandatory waiting periods.

The Solution

Instant slab scans instead of drilled probes and 24-hour waits

Handheld device placed on the slab surface
Electromagnetic fields measure moisture at depth
Full slab moisture map, no drilling, no waiting
